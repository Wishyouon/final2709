Summarize YouTube Transcripts Quickly and Easily

YouTube Transcript Summarizer User Guide

The YouTube Transcript Summarizer allows you to quickly and easily get a summarized version of a YouTube video's transcript. You can then copy the transcript, download it, or listen to it.

Getting a Summarized Transcript

  1. Go to the YouTube video you want to summarize.
  2. Click the "Share" button below the video.
  3. Click the "Copy" button to copy the video's URL.
  4. Paste the URL into the YouTube Transcript Summarizer.
  5. Select the desired percentage for the summary, such as 10%, 20%, or 30%.
  6. Click the "Summarize" button.
  7. Wait for the summarization process to complete.
  8. View the summarized transcript.

Copying the Transcript

To copy the transcript, simply select the text and use the copy function on your device.

Downloading the Transcript

To download the transcript, click the "Download" button and save the file to your device.

Listening to the Transcript

To listen to the transcript, click the "Listen" button and the summarized transcript will be read out loud to you.
